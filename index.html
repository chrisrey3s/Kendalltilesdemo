Quick Answers

Questions We Hear Every Day

How fast can you start?

In most cases, we can send an estimator within 24–48 hours of your first call. If it's urgent, let us know — we have crews available for next-day service.

Do you handle the installation too, or just supply the tile?

We do both — and that's the point. We supply the materials and our own licensed, insured crew handles the installation. One company, one bill, no coordination stress.

How much does installation cost?

It depends on the scope — square footage, material type, and surface prep. That's exactly why we offer free estimates. We measure your space and give you a clear, itemized quote with no surprise fees.

Are your installers licensed and insured?

Yes. Every installer on our team is licensed, fully insured, bonded, and background-checked. We take it seriously — you're letting us into your home.

Do you work on commercial properties?

Absolutely. We handle commercial and residential projects of all sizes — apartments, retail spaces, offices, hotels, and more. Commercial-grade materials are available.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Miami-Dade County within a 20-mile radius of our Kendall location at 8784 SW 133rd St. That covers Kendall, Coral Gables, Coconut Grove, Pinecrest, Palmetto Bay, Cutler Bay, and dozens more communities.

Can you do pool tile and outdoor pavers?

Yes — pool tile, driveway pavers, patio pavers, and all outdoor flooring is part of what we do. We use materials rated for Florida's outdoor conditions: heat, humidity, and pool chemicals.

Do you offer bathroom remodeling beyond just the tile?

Yes. We offer full bathroom remodeling services — not just tile work. If you want a complete transformation, we can handle the whole project from start to finish.
